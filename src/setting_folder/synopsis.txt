The story takes place in the kingdom of 
Elae, where we follow a group of heroes 
serving King Edgar who has been 
watching over the kingdom for many years. 
Following a surprise attack from the
neighboring kingdom of Aixus, our
heroes set off on an adventure seeking
vengeance against the kingdom responsible
for this massacre. You will follow the
adventures of our group of heroes and the
challenges that await them in this 
adventure full of twists and turns!
